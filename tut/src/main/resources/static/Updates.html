TNEA 2024 is a complete online process including Registration, Payments, Choice Filling, Allotment, and Confirmation.

Candidates are requested to read the instructions carefully for a successful counseling and admission process.

Important Advisory

The TNEA team will never contact students asking for usernames and passwords or suggesting college names. Be aware of fraudulent calls. If you need assistance, visit a nearby TFC center or contact us via email or phone.
